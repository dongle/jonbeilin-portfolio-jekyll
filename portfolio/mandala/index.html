Produced in one weekend as part of TIGJam 3, I wanted to create a touch-driven experience to encompass some of the ritual and physicality along with the temporality of the creation of sand mandalas. Trace the constituent shapes of a mandala one by one then shake the device to erase it. Over 14k downloads and used at home and in temples around the world.

And now it's open source on GitHub
