DIY.org App 1.X Signup – Design & Engineering

In collaboration with Isaiah Saxon, Zach Klein, and Andrew Sliwinski

At DIY.org, an educational community for kids aged 8-13, we faced the challenge of being many of our users' first social network, which meant that we couldn't take the signup process for granted. Many first time users did not understand why they should create an account, and got lost in the process when they were asked to do things like come up with a password. Moreover, we needed kids to come up with good passwords (eg, not password1) to protect their privacy. Since we took privacy and consent seriously, we didn't let kids participate until they were registered and approved by a parent; encouraging users to complete registration was critical.
