Ah, P*ngVaders. A light-hearted mashup of some beloved arcade games by a collaboration between four friends: Cole Krumbholz and I performed design and engineering, Anna Anthropy clicked the pixel art, and Jeremiah Johnson twiddled knobs for the soundtrack. As a member of the first wave of multiplayer games on the iPad, the game was a front-runner in the local multiplayer renaissance. It was a success, as well: we had nearly 100k downloads, putting us in the iPad top 20 (this was the early days, after all). We were featured by Apple as part of the 'Lost In Space' (now 'Galactic Games') app collection. We even had a few fans, including the late Kenji Eno.
