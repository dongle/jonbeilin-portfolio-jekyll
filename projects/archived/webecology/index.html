--- layout: project title: Web Ecology Project subtitle: Founding Researcher year: 2008-2010 ---

An informal, interdisplinary group of researchers, the Web Ecology Project produced timely reports regarding events on Twitter, balancing qualitative and quantitative methodologies. Papers included The Iranian Election On Twitter and sentiment analysis of tweets following Michael Jackon's death. We received press coverage from the likes of The New York Times, Foreign Policy, the New Yorker, and more.
