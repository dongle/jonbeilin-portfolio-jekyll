--- layout: project title: Eddie Murphy year: 2014 ---

Eddie Murphy is one of the biggest performers of all time. His career has spanned stand-up, acting, and music. During his three decade career, he's played almost every imaginable character archetype. And he's been on the periphery of some of the most important social topics including race, masculinity, and sexuality. In his honor, I'm watching all of his films and illustrating his character(s) in each one.

To be compiled into a book with an essay exploring Eddie Murphy's effect on pop culture.
